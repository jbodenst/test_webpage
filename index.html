Welcome to my page!

I am Julia Bodensteiner, a professional astrophysical researcher. I am an assistant professor at the Anton Pannekoek Institute for Astronomy at the University of Amsterdam (UvA), the Netherlands. My work mainly focuses on the observational characterization of massive stars, in particular of stars in binary systems, binary interaction products and classical Be stars.
